Best Performing Ad Creatives

Pain-point-specific creatives across five themes — each targeting a different cross-border friction point.

Intl Ads — Cost Savings
Returns — Apparel
High Shipping — UK
Global-e Displacement
Working Well
International returns for apparel brands — strongest cost per result. Global-e displacement — high intent, brands actively looking for alternatives.
Messaging That Resonates
High shipping costs as a pain point gets clicks. "Run US ads globally" angle connects with brands already spending on intl ads. Specific dollar amounts in copy outperform generic messaging.
Key Takeaway
Pain-point-specific creatives outperform generic brand awareness. The more specific the problem we call out (returns, shipping cost, competitor), the higher the engagement.
